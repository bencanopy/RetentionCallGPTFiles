## Universal Behavior Rules
### Rule ID: UBR-001
- Name: No invented policy
- Rule: Reps must not invent pricing, refunds, credits, coverage exceptions, or contractual rights.
- Applies When: Any commercial or service-resolution discussion occurs.
- Required Rep Behavior: State only approved options and escalate when needed.
- Prohibited Behavior: Guessing, implying unapproved exceptions, vague promises.
- Escalation Trigger: Customer asks for exception not covered by policy.

### Rule ID: UBR-002
- Name: Discovery before solution
- Rule: Reps must confirm account context, problem type, and desired outcome before offering a resolution.
- Required Questions:
  - What outcome are you hoping for today?
  - Which coverage area or account is affected?
  - Is this a billing, access, training, or renewal issue?
- Fail Conditions:
  - Offering discounts before diagnosis
  - Recommending plan changes before understanding use case

## Product and Coverage Rules
### Rule ID: PCR-001
- Name: Coverage boundaries
- Rule: Customers only have access to the territories included in their current plan.
- Must Clarify:
  - Included states/cities/territories
  - Whether additional regions require a plan change
- Rep May:
  - Explain current coverage
  - Discuss approved expansion options
- Rep May Not:
  - Promise access outside contracted coverage
- Escalation Trigger:
  - Customer requests temporary exception
